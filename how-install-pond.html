In this blog i will help you step by step in installing a pond. Make sure you have all the required materials listed above, I have links to my favorites.

Step One

Find a good place to put the pond. You dont want your pond near trees, and electrical lines. You also want to pick a spot that has level land, and soft soil. Make sure the location you pick does not stop people from walking.

Step Two

Place the pond insert upside down and trace it with the spray paint into the ground. This will give you an outline to follow when digging. Make sure to give the paint some time to dry so it does not wipe off.
